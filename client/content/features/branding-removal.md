## Present a fully branded form experience

Branding removal lets you hide the default OpnForm footer from public forms. Respondents see your logo, colors, and messaging without a platform badge at the bottom of the page.

::feature-callout
---
eyebrow: White-label forms
title: Why teams remove OpnForm branding
icon: i-heroicons-eye-slash
tone: emerald
image: /img/pages/features/branding-removal-hero.png
items:
  - Cleaner forms for customer-facing and public campaigns
  - A more professional look for sales, support, and onboarding flows
  - Better alignment with brand guidelines and white-label expectations
  - Stronger trust when every touchpoint feels owned by your company
---
Branding removal is available on the Pro plan and above. Self-hosted Enterprise customers can also enable it through the whitelabel license capability.
::

## What changes on your forms

When branding removal is enabled for a form:

- **Public form pages**: The OpnForm footer is hidden from the respondent view.
- **Embeds**: Embedded forms stay visually consistent with your site without a platform badge.
- **Your branding stays**: Logo, cover image, colors, and custom copy remain under your control.

## Remove OpnForm branding

::feature-workflow
---
title: Remove OpnForm branding from a form
steps:
  - title: Open form customization
    description: Edit your form and go to the customization section in the form builder.
  - title: Enable Hide OpnForm Branding
    description: Turn on the branding removal toggle in the Branding area of form settings.
  - title: Add your logo and theme
    description: Upload your logo, set cover styling, and adjust colors so the form feels fully on-brand.
  - title: Publish and share
    description: Share or embed the form knowing respondents will not see the OpnForm footer.
---
::

## Best practices

- Pair branding removal with your logo and cover image for a polished first impression.
- Use custom domains alongside branding removal for a fully owned respondent journey.
- Preview the public form before launching high-traffic campaigns.
- Keep brand assets consistent across forms, emails, and landing pages.
