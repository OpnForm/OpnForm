## Save progress before the final submit

Partial submissions let OpnForm capture in-progress responses when someone starts a form but does not complete it. You get visibility into drop-off points and can retain useful data instead of losing it entirely.

::feature-callout
---
eyebrow: Drop-off insights
title: Why teams enable partial submissions
icon: i-heroicons-clipboard-document-list
tone: amber
items:
  - See where respondents abandon long or multi-step forms
  - Recover contact details and early answers before completion
  - Improve conversion by fixing friction in high-drop fields
  - Track partial activity alongside completed submissions in analytics
---
Partial submissions are available on the Business plan and above.
::

## What gets captured

When partial submissions are enabled on a form:

- **In-progress answers**: Data entered before the respondent leaves or closes the form.
- **Drop-off insight**: Partial records help you understand which steps lose attention.
- **Submission stats**: Partial counts can appear in form analytics alongside completed submissions.
- **Final submission flow**: Completed submissions still work normally when the respondent finishes the form.

## Configure partial submissions

::feature-workflow
---
title: Turn on partial submissions
steps:
  - title: Open submission settings
    description: Edit your form and go to the submission settings section in the form builder.
  - title: Enable Collect partial submissions
    description: Turn on the partial submissions toggle under advanced submission options.
  - title: Publish the form
    description: Share or embed the form so respondents can start filling it out as usual.
  - title: Review partial and completed data
    description: Monitor partial submissions in your submissions table and analytics to spot drop-off trends.
---
::

## Best practices

- Enable partial submissions on longer forms where abandonment is most likely.
- Review partial data regularly to improve question order and field complexity.
- Combine partial submission insights with form analytics for a fuller picture.
- Be transparent with respondents if you collect in-progress data, especially for sensitive fields.
