## Put your brand on every form link

Custom domains let you replace generic hosted URLs with links that match your company, product, or campaign. Respondents see a polished, trustworthy experience from the first click through submission.

Custom domains are available on paid OpnForm plans and can also be configured for self-hosted deployments at the instance level.
::

## What you can customize

OpnForm supports custom domains at different levels depending on your deployment:

- **Cloud plans**: Attach a custom domain to your workspace forms so public links use your brand.
- **Self-hosted instances**: Point your instance URL to your own domain with DNS and environment configuration.
- **Form distribution**: Share branded links, embeds, and QR codes that stay consistent across channels.

## Configure your custom domain

::feature-workflow
---
title: Connect your custom domain
steps:
  - title: Choose the domain or subdomain
    description: Decide whether forms should live on a dedicated subdomain like forms.yourcompany.com or another branded URL.
  - title: Configure DNS records
    description: Point the domain to OpnForm using the DNS instructions provided in your workspace or deployment settings.
  - title: Verify and secure the domain
    description: Complete domain verification and ensure HTTPS is active before sharing the new links publicly.
  - title: Publish forms on the branded URL
    description: Share, embed, and distribute forms using the custom domain so every touchpoint stays on-brand.
---
::

## Best practices

- Use a short, memorable subdomain that is easy to communicate internally.
- Verify SSL before launching high-traffic or customer-facing forms.
- Keep DNS changes documented so your team can troubleshoot quickly.
- Pair custom domains with branded themes and logo settings for a cohesive experience.
