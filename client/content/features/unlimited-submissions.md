Unlimited submissions means you can publish forms and keep collecting answers as your audience grows. There are no per-form or per-account submission quotas holding back launches, campaigns, or everyday workflows.

## Best practices

- Use clear form titles and confirmation messages so respondents know their submission went through.
- Monitor submissions regularly during high-traffic campaigns even though there is no submission cap.
- Pair unlimited collection with notifications or integrations so your team can respond quickly.
- Upgrade when you need advanced features like custom domains or branding removal, not for more submissions.
